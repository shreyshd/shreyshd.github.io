Our Story

Because of COVID-19, many events are not able to occur in-person. Therefore, we created this mental health website to make an online experience of what we would've done in person! We included a "Problems" page to explain a some of the more common types of mental health disorders, and created Google Forms so you can take tests. In the "Solutions" page, we provided a general description of common practices to help with mental health issues, and gave access to websites and videos to learn more. We included future dates for online workshops so you can talk about your experiences with our team!

Threats of Mental Health

Mental Health doesn't discriminate. In fact, about 26% of Americans who are 18 or older in age have some sort of mental disorder
(John Hopkins 2022). Mental health not only causes major depressive moods, but can also cause serious consequences, such as
suicides. For example, many people who committed suicide are victims of some type of mental disorder, which can be diagnosable
(John Hopkins 2022).
